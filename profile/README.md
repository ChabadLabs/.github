# ChabadLabs

AI for Shluchim. Secure, open-source, and built for the future of your shlichus.

Your AI-powered command center — tools, resources, and a community of builders transforming shlichus with cutting-edge technology.

## What We Do

**Tools & Resources** — 60+ curated AI tools, from ChatGPT to design tools to Jewish-specific resources.

**Workshops** — Learn directly from community experts. Recorded sessions covering basics, advanced tools, and deep dives into Nanoclaw.

**Fund Builders** — We fund shluchim who build. If you have a working project or a solid idea, apply for a grant.

## GabayAI

A personal Shlichus AI assistant that runs on WhatsApp, powered by [Nanoclaw](https://github.com/qwibitai/nanoclaw). GabayAI helps shluchim with scheduling, answering questions, managing WhatsApp groups, voice transcription, and more.

**[Request access](https://github.com/ChabadLabs/.github/issues/new?template=access-request.yml)** to get started.

## Nanoclaw: Secure AI Agents

Deploy your own AI agents with enterprise-grade security. Nanoclaw isolates each chat in its own Docker container — preconfigured packages for shluchim.

Learn more at [nanoclaw.dev](https://nanoclaw.dev).
